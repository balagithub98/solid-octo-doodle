Who We Are

PallyFlo is a Canadian multinational agro-commodities exporter based in Toronto, Ontario, delivering pulses and seeds worldwide by sourcing from farmers, processors, and wholesalers.

What We Do

We connect the core of the global supply chain sustainably and ethically, using deep market knowledge to manage supply chains and ensure resource viability.

Our Mission

To better the world by enabling international trade of goods and services through high-quality products, logistics, and services.

Our Vision

To be recognized as one of the most reliable and prosperous commercial enterprises globally.

Our Values

  • Global Responsibility: Making decisions that benefit society, respecting human dignity, and supporting sustainable development.
  • Integrity and Transparency: Upholding honesty and openness across all business operations.
  • Pursue Excellence: Continuously exceeding expectations and fostering innovation.
