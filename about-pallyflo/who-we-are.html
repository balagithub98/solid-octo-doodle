About Our Identity

PallyFlo is a Canadian multinational agro-commodities exporter based in Toronto, Ontario. We export pulses, oil seeds, cereals, and specialty crops to global markets, sourcing from farmers, processors, and wholesalers.

Our expertise connects the most vital points in the supply chain while ensuring sustainability and ethical practices.
